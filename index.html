Method and Processing

  • All CSV fields are parsed to numbers and sorted by year before rendering.
  • Linked interactions are driven by a shared application state for year, background group, and education level.
  • Derived indicators combine datasets to explain change, not just display raw values.
  • Education is linked by year using the national tertiary-rate series available in the dataset.

Data Notes

The dashboard checks year coverage across datasets and states where fallback values are used.

Source files: population_year.csv, citizenship_year.csv, labour_share_year.csv, and education_year.csv.
